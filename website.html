Meet The Doctor That Transformed Many Lives

K. Douglas Barker, DMD, is a cosmetic and general dentist at Newport Center Smile Design in Newport Beach, California. He is most passionate about implant dentistry and uses his surgical and prosthetic skill set to restore people’s smiles in a conservative and non-invasive way. At Newport Center Smile Design, he performs the majority of oral and dental implant surgeries himself, and offers Invisalign®, full mouth reconstructions, and custom veneers.

Dr. Barker decided at a very young age that he wanted to be a dentist. As an orthodontic patient from the age of seven, he remembers the warm compassion he received from his orthodontist and their staff, which left a great impression on him. Today, he strives to exhibit the same compassion for his patients.

Why Should You Get Dental Implants?

If you’re looking for a way to improve your confidence in day to day interactions, or you’re sick of dealing with denture limitations - we have the best solution for you.

Dental implants are a permanent solution that has the same functionality as regular teeth. The titanium implant gets infused into the bone and functions as well as natural teeth.

Most dental implant inquiries that we receive have suffered from both emotional and physical pain.

If you can relate, we want to help you!

Our Dental Implant restorations are created right here in the U.S.A. with precision technology and craftsmanship, ensuring an authentic solution that gives our patients the healthy mouth and beautiful teeth they’ve been longing for.

The newfound comfort, renewed confidence, and ease of maintenance you'll enjoy with Dental Implants will transform your quality of life for many years to come.

Explore Our Dental Implant Options

Whether you're considering a lower jaw or an upper jaw full arch dental implant bridge, or maybe even full arch full-mouth restoration, we have the solution that will meet your individual needs.

Every restoration solution includes a finely crafted prosthetic restoration supported by permanent titanium dental implants.

By attaching permanently to the implants instead of the gums or natural teeth, full arch dental implant solutions eliminate the many problems associated with conventional treatments.

Because everyone is different, we don't just offer one dental implant option.

Our Dental Implant doctors believe the right treatment is the one that's best for the patient, and so there is more than just one dental solution available.

In fact - there are three and one for every budget!
